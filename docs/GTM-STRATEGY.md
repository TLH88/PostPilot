# PostPilot Go-to-Market Strategy

## Launch Pricing Strategy
- **Founder pricing:** 40% off for first 500 customers (builds testimonials, early data)
- **Early adopter program:** Invite alpha testers to Creator tier free for 3 months
- Launch with **Free + Creator + Professional** tiers only; add Business tier in Q2

## Marketing Channels
1. **Dog-fooding:** Use PostPilot to create LinkedIn content about PostPilot
2. **Referral program:** $20 credit for each referred paying customer
3. **LinkedIn creator community partnerships:** Promotions with micro-influencers
4. **Content marketing:** Case studies from early users showing before/after content quality

## Key Metrics to Track
1. Signup to trial conversion rate (target: 8-12%)
2. Trial to paid conversion rate (target: 15-20%)
3. Free to Creator conversion rate (target: 5-10%)
4. Monthly churn rate (target: <8%)
5. CAC and LTV by channel
6. Feature usage by tier (guides product priorities)

## Ad Revenue (Free Tier)
- Free tier users see third-party ads (e.g., Google AdSense) in sidebar, dashboard, and between content sections
- Paid tiers (Creator, Professional) are completely ad-free
- Ad revenue supplements subscription revenue and helps offset infrastructure costs for free users
- Estimated CPM: $2-5 for B2B/professional audience (LinkedIn creators are a high-value demographic)

## Revenue Targets
- Monthly operating costs: ~$130/mo ($25 Supabase + $100 AI coding platform + ~$5.42 domain)
- Break-even target: $150/mo (costs + 15% profit margin)
- Additional revenue stream: ad impressions from free tier users
- Conservative Year 1: $25K ARR (~300 paying customers)
- Moderate Year 1: $75K ARR (~600 paying customers)
- Optimistic Year 1: $150K ARR (~1,000 paying customers)

## Positioning
- **Primary:** "The only LinkedIn tool that keeps YOUR voice authentically you"
- **For solo creators:** "Your voice, amplified"
- **For privacy-conscious:** "Your AI, your provider, your data"
- **Key differentiator:** BYOK model means lower cost than competitors + user controls their AI

## Retention Strategies
1. **Habit formation:** AI feedback loop (better posts -> more engagement -> more use)
2. **Switching costs:** Profile data, post history, creator voice locked in
3. **Community:** Newsletter with tips, templates, creator stories
4. **Regular updates:** Feature releases, templates, analytics improvements

## Psychological Pricing Triggers
- Annual discount (17% off) encourages commitment
- Freemium reduces friction, drives volume
- Clear upgrade path: each tier solves a specific pain point
- Yearly price points ($190, $490) are more memorable than monthly
